Our Story

A Fellow Student
Who Truly Cares

My name is Senzo Mnguni, a young South African from Hluhluwe, KwaZulu-Natal, currently pursuing a Bachelor of Education majoring in Mathematics and Life Sciences FET at Sol Plaatje University in Kimberley, Northern Cape.

As a student myself, I witnessed firsthand how thousands of talented young South Africans miss their opportunity to study — not because they lack the ability, but because they miss deadlines, misread prospectuses, don't know how to check if they qualify, or simply don't understand the application process.

That's why I founded EduConnect Services — to bridge the gap between students and their future. Behind EduConnect is not a big corporation, but a fellow student who understands your struggles and is genuinely invested in seeing you succeed.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

Is my personal information safe?▾

Yes, 100%. All your personal information and documents are handled with strict confidentiality and only used for submitting your applications. We never share your data with anyone.

How long does it take?▾

Once we receive your completed form, documents and proof of payment, we aim to submit your applications within 24 to 48 hours and send you confirmation with reference numbers.

What if I don't get accepted?▾

EduConnect ensures your application is correctly submitted. Admission decisions rest with the institutions. We always recommend applying to multiple universities to improve your chances.

Do you help students outside KZN?▾

Absolutely! We serve students across all 9 provinces. Everything is done online via WhatsApp and email — no need to be in the same city or province as us.

When should I contact you?▾

As early as possible! Applications are currently open and closing dates range from July to September. Don't wait — contact us now to secure your spot before it's too late!

How do I pay?▾

We accept EFT and Cash Send to our Capitec Bank account. Use your ID number as the payment reference and send us proof of payment via WhatsApp to confirm your booking.
